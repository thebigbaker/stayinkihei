Drive to Hana

Start this drive early. From our condo, you probably want to be on the road by 6:00am and the earlier the better. There are a lot of guides out there and we've always used the guide in the book "Maui Revealed," which we have a copy in our condos. "Maui Revealed" is the best Maui guide book out there and they have a great guide to follow for your drive to Hana. Take your time on the drive, as there are many places to pull off to take pictures and several waterfalls along the way.

As you near Hana, definitely make sure you stop at Wai'anapanapa Black Sand Beach. This is another one of those Maui locations that you'll want to have plenty of memory on your camera card. Don't forget to use the coolers in the condo to pack a lunch for you trip to Hana.

On your way to Hana, many guides will tell you to stop at the “Halfway to Hana” snack shop for the banana bread and for good reason. The banana bread is very good and I recommend stopping there on the way to Hana and on the way back to Kihei!

Many guests have bought and used CD guides for their trip to Hana and some of those CDs have been left in our condos. We leave these in the TV cabinets so grab one for a voice guided tour if you like.
