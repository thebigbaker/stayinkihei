Old Lahaina Luau

If you are looking to attend a Luau, this is it. Not just the best luau on Maui, but the best luau in all of Hawaii. The show is a traditional story telling Hawaiian luau, so there's not any flame throwing or crazy theatrics (not that those are bad).

Even without the over-the-top flare, the show is very entertaining and authentic. The food is great and all you can eat. The open bar starts as you enter the luau, as they hand you a drink on your way in. The service is second to none and they will ensure that you have a great time.

Located on Front Street behind the Cannery Mall next door to Aloha Mixed Plate. For more information: www.oldlahainaluau
