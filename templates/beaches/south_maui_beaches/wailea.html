Wailea Beach

Whenever you see pictures of movies stars on the beach in Maui, this is usually the beach they visit (mainly because they tend to stay at the Grand Wailea). Great swimming beach with good snorkeling and body boarding on the south side (left) when the surf is up. Since there are a few large resorts at this beach (Grand Wailea and Four Seasons), it can get crowded here.

Swimming can be great and when the swell is up, body boarding can be good also. Snorkeling can be good on both ends but we prefer the south side (left side) of the beach as there is a very large reef here. It can be hit or miss depending on how much sand is being kicked up by the surf, but if you are a good swimmer, you can really go out far on the south end reef as it extends hundreds of yards out and gets much more clear compared to closer to the shore. Some tour boats will anchor off this reef and let out snorkelers and divers to swim this area (I've snorkeled out to these areas several times, but please only do this is you are a good swimmer and experienced snorkeler! We've seen lots of fish, including some big trevally and several turtles.
