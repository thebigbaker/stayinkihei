Ulua Beach

Great snorkeling at this beach! The north side (right side) has a great shallow coral formation with lots of fish and several turtles. We've run into a few octopuses, eels, lobsters, manta rays and we always run into a couple of turtles here. (A tip for spotting turtles at most beaches is to snorkel around the edges of the reef. We've seen most of our turtles on the reef edge and it doesn't matter how far out you are, we've seen them just a few feet from shore so keep a look out!)

The lava formation on the right also creates a great tide pool area. Our daughter loves playing in the tide pools where there are lots of fish, star fish, urchins, hermit crabs and even a few baby eels living in the tide pools.
