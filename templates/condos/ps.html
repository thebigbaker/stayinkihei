Pacific Shores Condo Information

Our new Pacific Shores condo features beautiful ocean and mountain views from the lanai and the bedroom. Pacific Shores is located across the street from Charley Young Beach/ Kamaole I Beach, which is a long stretch of beach with soft sand, a gentle slope into the water and excellent snorkeling and swimming.

Our fully equipped kitchen with new quartz countertops, microwave, coffee maker, blender, toaster and all cookware, glassware and utensils offers the convenience to prepare full meals and store leftovers from neighboring restaurants.

Comfortable furnishings include a queen sized bed in the bedroom with soft, luxurious sheets, a new queen sized sleeper sofa with a comfortable memory foam mattress, a full bathroom with a new Delta In2ition 2-in-1 shower head with an easy to use detachable hand shower and fresh clean linens and towels.

The living area is equipped with a LED HD TV and free cable television. Other free services include wired and wireless broadband internet and free parking.

To help you pack less and make your beach excursions more enjoyable, we have a complete set of beach gear available for your use. Beach chairs, coolers, beach umbrella, sand toys for the keikis, beach towels, and beach bags are ready for you to take to the beach.

The Pacific Shores pool/ hot tub/ grill area is a great place to sit and relax or enjoy an outdoor meal with one of the several gas grills. Surrounding Pacific Shores are several restaurants, cafes and shops all within walking distance.
