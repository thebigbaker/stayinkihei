Kihei Kai Nani Condo Information


Our 1 bed/ 1 bath, 621 sqft ground floor condo features a relaxing 4 poster queen size bed with high thread count Egyptian cotton sheets. Our fully equipped kitchen with a microwave, coffee maker, blender, toaster, all cookware, glassware and utensils offers the convenience to prepare full meals and store leftovers from neighboring restaurants. The recently remodeled bathroom features a new Delta In2ition 2-in-1 shower head with an easy to use detachable hand shower.

The LED HD TV and free cable television will ensure you don't miss the big game during your vacation. The Blu-ray DVD player and free Netflix streaming service provide a large library of movies and TV shows plus the large selection of Netflix kid friendly shows will sure to be a hit with the keikis. Other free services include wired and wireless broadband internet, streaming Pandora Radio, local telephone and 411 information services.

Comfortable furnishings include a comfortable queen size sofa sleeper, ceiling fans in the living area and bedroom, cool tile floors and fresh clean linens and towels.

To help you pack less and make your beach excursions more enjoyable, we have a complete set of beach gear available for your use. Beach chairs, coolers, beach umbrella, beach mats, sand toys, beach towels, beach bag and body boards are ready for you to take to the beach.

The Kihei Kai Nani is nestled across the street from Kamaole II Beach, which is a long stretch of beach with soft sand, a gentle slope into the water and excellent snorkeling and swimming. Come sundown, Kamaole II Beach is a picturesque spot to view breathtaking sunsets. Surrounding the Kihei Kai Nani are several restaurants, cafes and shops all within walking distance.

Check-Out:

11:00am Hawaiian time with late check-out options available.

Keys:

Our keys are located in a lock box on our front door and the key code will be provided to guests prior to their arrival.

Internet Connections

Free interenet access is provided with our condo with both wired and wireless options available. Wireless interenet connection password and information will be provided to guests prior to their arrival.

Laundry:

Although we do not have a washer and dryer inside our condo, there is a coin operated laundry facility connected to the Kihei Kai Nani office, which is right across the parking lot from our condo. At this moment, a large load to wash is $3 per load and 25 cents per 15 minutes of drying time.

Pool:

The large 60' x 30' Kihei Kai Nani pool is one of the largest fresh water pools in the area.

Parking:

Parking is free for Kihei Kai Nani guests. Each guest will receive a parking pass for their car upon checking in with the office. The parking lot runs down the middle of the complex, so there will be ample amount of parking close to our condo.
